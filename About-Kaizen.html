Kaizen Vision

“To become a center of excellence in the field of Gastroenterology in India which is recognized globally, entailing the finest medical facilities combined with compassionate care.”

Kaizen Mission

“To contribute to the well being of the patients by creating high standards of care, utilizing the best technology, continuous innovation, ensuring complete patient satisfaction & encouraging research and education in the field of Gastroenterology”

Kaizen Value

We at Kaizen Hospital Ahmedabad hold these values to be fundamental :-

  • Treat everyone in our diverse community, including patients, their families and our staff with dignity & respect.
  • Dealing with patients and their families with sensitivity and compassion.
  • Maintain patient’s information privacy.

    NABH Accreditation First Gastroenterology Super Speciality Hospital of Western India

    With the aim of creating a world-class infrastructure for Treatment of Gastroenterology in Ahmedabad, dedicated team of doctors decided to embark on this project for giving finest care to the patients with complaints of GI diseases.
