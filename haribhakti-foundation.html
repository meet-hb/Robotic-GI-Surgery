About Haribhakti Education Foundation

Welcome to Kaizen-Gujarat’s First and exclusive hospital specialized in the field of Gastroenterology. With a spectrum of advanced facilities and a team that extends compassion along with care, Kaizen is adapt to deal with every problems related to the digestive system under one roof. The hospital also has on board the largest in-house full time team of highly experienced doctors. Add to it, our continuous effort to finding better medical solutions to provide patients with a superior level of care.

Kaizen is way of life” Japanese” philosophy that every aspect of life deserves to be continuously improved. Inspired by this philosophy, we are working towards creating a better & a healthier world. Join us on this journey of care , compassion & cure……..

Dr.K.S.Patel

CEO of Haribhakti Education Foundation

Dr. K. S. PatelMS, FICSAn alumnus of M. P. Shah Medical College Jamnagar, his keen interest in G. I. Surgery took him to U. K. where he worked at Digestive disease center – Leeds General Infirmary, Leeds U. K.At present Director, Department of Surgical Gastroenterology, KAIZEN HOSPITAL INSTITUTE OF GASTROENTEROLOGY & RESEARCH CENTRE, Helmet Circle, 132ft Ring Road, Memnagar, Ahmedabad.

He is incharge of liver and pancreatic speciality clinic His KAIZEN philosophy took him to centers of excellence to enlarge his knowledge and learn advanced skills in his field National Cancer Centre Tokyo KyungpookNational University South Korea Universityhospital Heidelberg Germany Memorial Sloan Kettering in NewyorkHe is interested in HPB surgery in holds extensive experience in complex surgeries like Liver Resection, Surgery of Bile duct injury and Shunt surgery. He has conducted surgery for Chronic & acute pancreatitis on many a patients.He recently awarded fellowship at Kings College hospital, London in Dept of HPB surgery & Liver Transplantation.
