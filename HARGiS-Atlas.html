HARGiS Atlas

“Dr. Haribhakti has significantly contributed to medical education through the creation of HARGiS - Dr. Haribhakti’s Atlas of Robotic GI Surgery. This innovative online platform provides a comprehensive collection of high-quality operative videos for robotic gastrointestinal surgery procedures.”

200+

Successful Surgery

Key features of HARGiS include

  • Structured Learning: The videos are organized in a structured manner that facilitates easy learning of robotic operative procedures.
  • High-Definition Quality: All videos are edited using HD software for clarity and professionalism.
  • Detailed Content: Each video includes case specifics, port placements, and a step-by- step breakdown of procedures, followed by patient outcomes.
  • Annotations and Commentary: Important surgical landmarks are highlighted, and expert commentary enriches the learning experience.
  • Global Accessibility: The platform aims to make unrestricted robotic surgery education accessible and affordable worldwide, promoting standardized techniques and advanced training.
  • This initiative reflects a dedication to enhancing surgical education and is poised to advance the field of robotic surgery globally.
